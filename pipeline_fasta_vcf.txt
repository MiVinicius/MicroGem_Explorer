Instalar o conda

wget https://github.com/conda-forge/miniforge/releases/latest/download/Miniforge3-Linux-x86_64.sh



Criar o ambiente conda

conda create -n fasta2vcf minimap2 samtools bcftools python -y
conda activate fasta2vcf

Colocar ref.fasta, sample.fasta e o script.py na mesma pasta e rodar o .py!
